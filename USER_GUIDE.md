# Emergencize User Guide

**Welcome to Emergencize!** This guide will help you understand how to use the emergency alert system to stay connected and protected.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Website**
   - Open Emergencize in your web browser
   - Click the **"Get Started"** button on the home page

2. **Sign Up**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password
   - Click **"Sign Up"** to create your account

3. **Sign In**
   - If you already have an account, click **"Sign In"**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click **"Sign In"** to access your dashboard

## Adding Emergency Contacts

Your emergency contacts are the people who will receive your alerts. Here's how to add them:

### Step 1: Open Contact Manager
- Go to your **Dashboard**
- Click the **"Contacts"** or **"Add Contact"** button

### Step 2: Send Friend Request
- Enter your contact's email address
- Click **"Send Request"**
- Your contact will receive an invitation

### Step 3: Wait for Acceptance
- Your contact needs to have an Emergencize account
- They must accept your friend request
- Once accepted, they'll appear in your contacts list

### Step 4: Customize Contacts (Optional)
- Click on a contact to edit their details
- Add a **nickname** for easy identification
- Set their **relationship** (Family, Friend, Coworker, etc.)
- Save your changes

### Managing Contacts
- **View Online Status**: Green dot = online, Gray dot = offline
- **Remove Contact**: Click the remove/delete button next to their name
- **Edit Details**: Click on a contact to update their information

## Sending Emergency Alerts

Emergencize has two types of emergency buttons:

### HELP Button (Blue)
**Use for non-critical situations**

- **When to Use**: Car trouble, lost wallet, need a ride, minor assistance
- **Who Gets Notified**: Only contacts who are currently online
- **How to Send**:
  1. Click the blue **"HELP"** button once
  2. Alert sends instantly
  3. Your location is shared automatically (if permitted)

### DANGER Button (Red)
**Use for life-threatening emergencies**

- **When to Use**: Medical emergency, accident, immediate danger, assault
- **Who Gets Notified**: ALL your contacts (both online and offline)
- **How to Send**:
  1. Press and **HOLD** the red **"DANGER"** button
  2. Keep holding for 3 seconds (prevents accidental sends)
  3. Watch the progress circle complete
  4. Alert sends to everyone with highest priority

### What Happens After You Send an Alert?

1. **Instant Notification**: Your contacts receive a notification immediately
2. **Location Shared**: Your GPS coordinates are included (if enabled)
3. **Visual Confirmation**: You'll see a confirmation message
4. **Alert History**: The alert is saved in your notification history

## Location Sharing

### Enabling Location Access

1. **Browser Prompt**
   - When you first use Emergencize, your browser will ask for location permission
   - Click **"Allow"** to enable location sharing

2. **Why It's Important**
   - Your contacts can see exactly where you are during an emergency
   - Helps first responders or friends find you quickly

3. **Privacy**
   - Location is only shared when YOU send an alert
   - Not tracked or stored when you're not sending alerts
   - You can revoke permission anytime in browser settings

### Checking Location Status

- Look for the location icon on your dashboard
- Green = Location enabled
- Red or crossed out = Location disabled

## Connection Status

### Understanding Connection Modes

Your dashboard shows your current connection status:

**Real-Time Mode** (Green)
- Fully connected to internet and servers
- Instant notifications
- All features available
- Best experience

**Standard Mode** (Yellow)
- Connected to internet
- Some delays possible
- All features work
- Alerts saved to database

**Offline Mode** (Red)
- No internet connection
- Alerts queued locally
- Will send when back online
- Limited features

### What to Do in Offline Mode

- Don't panic! The app still works
- Alerts are saved locally on your device
- They'll automatically send when connection returns
- Keep the browser tab open if possible

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Emergencize free to use?**
A: Yes, the basic emergency alert features are free for everyone.

**Q: Do my contacts need an account?**
A: Yes, your emergency contacts must create an Emergencize account to receive your alerts.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes! Emergencize works on any device with a web browser. You can add it to your home screen for easy access.

### Alert Questions

**Q: What's the difference between HELP and DANGER?**
A: HELP is for non-critical situations and sends to online contacts only. DANGER is for life-threatening emergencies and sends to ALL contacts.

**Q: Can I cancel an alert after sending?**
A: Once sent, an alert cannot be cancelled, but you can immediately send a follow-up message via chat to clarify.

**Q: How many alerts can I send?**
A: There's a rate limit to prevent accidental spam, but you can send multiple alerts if needed.

### Location Questions

**Q: Is my location always tracked?**
A: No! Your location is ONLY accessed and shared when YOU send an emergency alert.

**Q: What if I don't want to share my location?**
A: You can deny location permissions, but alerts without location may be less helpful to your contacts.

**Q: How accurate is the location?**
A: GPS accuracy is typically within 5-20 meters, depending on your device and environment.

### Contact Questions

**Q: How many contacts can I add?**
A: There's no strict limit, but we recommend 3-10 key emergency contacts.

**Q: Can I remove someone from my contacts?**
A: Yes, you can remove contacts anytime from the Contact Manager.

**Q: Will removed contacts know?**
A: No, there's no notification when you remove someone.

### Privacy Questions

**Q: Who can see my location?**
A: Only your approved emergency contacts, and only when you send an alert.

**Q: Is my data sold or shared?**
A: No, your personal information and location data are never sold or shared with third parties.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, you can request account deletion in Settings.

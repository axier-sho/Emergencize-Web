## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 3 December 2025

### 1. Who we are

This website (**emergencize.com**) is operated by **axier** (“we”, “us”, “our”).
Our organization website is **axier.dev**.

If you have questions, please contact us through the contact page on **emergencize.com**.

---

### 2. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1. **Information you give us directly**

   * Name or nickname
   * Other information you enter into forms
   * Messages you send through contact forms or feedback forms

2. **Information collected automatically**

   * IP address
   * Browser type (for example: Chrome, Safari)
   * Pages you visit and how long you stay
   * Device information (for example: phone, tablet, computer)

3. **Cookies and similar technologies**

   * We may use cookies to:

     * Remember your settings (like language)
     * Understand how people use the website
   * You can change cookie settings in your browser.

---

### 3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

* Provide, operate, and improve our website
* Respond to your questions or messages
* Analyze how people use the website (for example, which pages are popular)
* Protect the website from spam or abuse
* Follow the law if we must

We will **not** sell your personal information to other companies.

---

### 4. Legal basis (for users in the EU/EEA)

If you are in the European Union (EU) or EEA, we process your personal data based on:

* Your **consent** (for example, when you accept cookies or send a form)
* Our **legitimate interests** (for example, improving our website, protecting against abuse)
* **Legal obligations** (when we must follow the law)

---

### 5. How we share your information

We may share your information with:

* **Service providers** that help us run the website
  (for example: hosting company, analytics tools)
* **Authorities or law enforcement** if the law requires it

We try to share only the minimum information needed.

---

### 6. Cookies and analytics

We may use third-party tools (for example, analytics services) to understand how visitors use the website.

These tools may:

* Use cookies
* Collect usage data (pages visited, time on page, clicks, etc.)

You can:

* Turn off cookies in your browser settings
* Use “Do Not Track” or similar privacy settings (if available in your browser)

If you use a specific tool (for example, Google Analytics or others), you can add a sentence like:

> We use third-party analytics tools. These providers may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

---

### 7. How we protect your information

We try to keep your information safe by using:

* Technical measures (for example: secure connections using HTTPS)
* Organizational measures (for example: limiting who can access data)

However, **no method** of sending data over the internet is 100% secure.
We cannot promise perfect security, but we do our best.

---

### 8. How long we keep your information

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary:

* To provide our services
* To follow legal requirements
* To resolve disputes

When we no longer need the information, we will delete it or make it anonymous.

---

### 9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

* See what personal information we have about you
* Ask us to correct information that is wrong
* Ask us to delete your information
* Ask us to limit how we use your information
* Object to our use of your information
* Withdraw your consent (for example, turn off optional cookies)

If you want to use these rights, please contact us through the contact page on **emergencize.com**.
We may need to confirm your identity before we can help.

---

### 10. Children’s privacy

Our website is **not designed for children under 13 years old**.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without permission from a parent or guardian, we will try to delete it.

If you think a child has given us personal information, please contact us through the contact page on **emergencize.com**.
